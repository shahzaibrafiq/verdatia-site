The firm

True data.

Verdatia is coined from the Latin verus — true — and data. That's the whole promise, and we picked a name we'd have to live up to every month.

Mission

Run books and finance systems an executive can sign without a second look.

Vision

A back office where every number traces to source — and software does the busywork.

Working rules

How we hold ourselves.

  1. Claims are verifiable or absent. If we can't show it, we don't say it.

  2. Numbers over adjectives. "Closed on day four" beats "fast turnaround."

  3. Problems get named plainly. Bad news early is a service. Bad news late is a bill.

  4. A status email every Friday. Done, next, flags — whether or not you asked.

  5. Systems over heroics. If it only works because someone remembered, it doesn't work.

Founder
