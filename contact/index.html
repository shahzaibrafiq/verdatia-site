Before you write

Five questions we always get.

How do engagements start?

Every engagement starts with a free discovery session: tell us what's broken, we answer straight, and we quote fixed before any work begins. No charge to ask. If you already know what you want, you can skip straight to a $100 working session on your own files — the hour is work, not a pitch.

How do we pay you?

USD invoices, settled by US ACH or bank wire.

Do you sign tax filings?

No. We deliver tax-ready books and work alongside your CPA — they sign, we make sure there's nothing to argue about first.

What access do you need?

Accountant or read-only access to QuickBooks Online or Xero. We ask for the least access that lets us do the work.

What about time zones?

We keep a daily overlap with US and UK working hours. The Friday status email means you're never waiting on a time zone to find out where things stand.
