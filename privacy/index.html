Privacy

What we collect.

Short version: this site sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and tracks nobody. The only data we get is what you deliberately send us.

The contact form

If you use the form on our contact page, you send us your name, your email address, and your message. That submission is processed by Formspree, which forwards it to our inbox. We use it to answer you and, if we end up working together, to run the engagement. We don't sell it, rent it, or add you to a mailing list — we don't have a mailing list.

Fonts

Our typefaces load from Google Fonts. That means Google's servers receive your IP address when a page loads, as they would on any site using the service. We mention it because it's the one third party your browser contacts here, and we'd rather name it than let you find it in a network tab.

What we don't do

No cookies. No analytics. No advertising pixels, no session recording, no fingerprinting, no consent banner — because there's nothing to consent to. If that changes, this page changes first.

Hosting

This site is static and hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub keeps standard server logs, which we don't have access to and don't use.

Your data, your call

Write to contact@verdatia.com and ask what we hold on you, ask for a copy, or ask us to delete it. We'll do it, and we'll reply within one business day like everything else.
